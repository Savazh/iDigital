It is important to understand the concept of a brand in order to understand what branding exactly is. A brand is the "image" or reputation of the product or service that customer familiar with by identifying the logo, slogan, design, or name of the company. Branding is the process to market that "image" of a product or service so that it can be easily recognized in a competitive market. The process is about creating a unique image for a product in consumer’s mind. By creating a brand, you market a promise that explains what your customers should expect from your products and services.
